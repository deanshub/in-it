# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how in-it by nissix ("we" or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "users") of our web application ("the App"). We are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Personal Information Collected

We may collect the following personal information from users:

- Email address
- Avatar (profile picture)
- GitHub username
- Repository you work on
- Build information
- Bundles data

## 2. Collection Methods

We collect personal information through the following methods:

- Registration: When you create an account, we collect the necessary information to set up your user profile.
- Usage Analytics: We gather data on how you interact with the App to improve our services and user experience.
- GitHub Login: If you choose to log in with your GitHub account, we collect the information necessary for authentication and access to your repository data.

## 3. Use of Personal Information

The personal information we collect is used internally within the App for the following purposes:

- Providing personalized user experience
- Analyzing and improving the performance of web apps
- Generating insights and metrics related to bundles and build information

## 4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All data collected is used strictly for internal purposes within the App.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the lifetime of your account, or as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

## 6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is securely stored using the security measures provided by MongoDB Atlas.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies in the App.

## 8. Review and Update of Personal Information

If you would like to review, update, or delete the personal information we have collected about you, please send an email to support@nissix.com. We will provide you with the requested information and assist you with any necessary updates or deletions.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we may have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@nissix.com, and we will take appropriate measures to investigate and address the issue.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries regarding your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nissix.com.
